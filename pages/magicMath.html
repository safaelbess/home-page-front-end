Magic math is acourse that simulates the human brain by using three methods to perform the mathmatical operations
The Methods are Mental Method , Sorban Method an Fingures Method.
* Mental Method is the way of performing the mathmatical operations mentally without need to a calculator by dealing between the right and the left side of the brain
**Sorban Method is a chinese way depending on the counter beads .. every counter presents a number .the sorban is devided to some columns Ones , Tens and Hundreds
*** The fingures Method uses the fingures to compute the substraction and addition operations ..Every fingure represents a number..
